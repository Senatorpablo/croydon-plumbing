Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Croydon Plumbing Solutions ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, email address and phone number when you submit a contact form or request a quote
  • Address details when you book a service appointment
  • Payment information for invoicing purposes
  • Website usage data collected via cookies (see our Cookie Policy)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide quotes
  • Deliver the plumbing, heating or bathroom services you have requested
  • Communicate about appointments, scheduling and follow-ups
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve our website and services

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following lawful bases as defined by the UK GDPR:

  • Consent: when you submit a form or accept cookies
  • Contract: to fulfil our service agreement with you
  • Legal obligation: to comply with tax, health and safety regulations
  • Legitimate interest: to improve our services and communicate with you

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted third parties who assist in delivering our services (e.g. payment processors, suppliers) or when required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, typically no longer than 6 years for financial records and 2 years for marketing data.

7. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details.
